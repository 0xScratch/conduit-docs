# Get Sepolia ETH

To get Sepolia (testnet) ETH on your chain, you'll need to acquire some from a faucet then bridge them over.

### Steps

1. First, head to one of our recommended faucets
    - [Superchain Faucet](https://app.optimism.io/faucet) *(Requires GitHub verification)*
    - [Paradigm MultiFaucet](https://faucet.paradigm.xyz/) *(Requires Twitter sign in)*
    - [Alchemy Faucet](https://www.alchemy.com/faucets/ethereum-sepolia) *(Requires Alchemy account)*
2. Enter your wallet address and begin receiving funds
3. Once received, head to your deployment dashboard in the [Conduit app](https://app.conduit.xyz/)
4. Add your chain to your wallet's list of networks
5. Head to your chain's bridge
    - If you haven't created one yet, [follow this guide](https://docs.conduit.xyz/guides/bridge-ui)
6. Move your Sepolia ETH from the Ethereum Sepolia network to your new chain
