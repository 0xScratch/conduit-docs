# Deploy a Rollup

Deploying your own L2 or L3 with Conduit is quick and easy. It only takes a few minutes through the Conduit app, no code required.

1. Head to the [Conduit app](https://app.conduit.xyz)
2. Sign in or create an account
3. Click `+ New deployment` 
4. Select your ideal modular stack
5. Choose between a Testnet or Mainnet
6. Add your billing details
    - We charge a monthly fee per deployment
7. Deploy!
